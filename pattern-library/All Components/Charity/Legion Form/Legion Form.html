Are you Legion?

The Legion is the premier Volunteer Program sponsored by and representing CMON, whose goal is promoting the games that CMON 
publishes, such as Rum and Bones, Blood Rage, Zombicide, The Grizzled, Arcadia Quest, XenoShyft, and our miniatures lines such as Dark
Age and Wrath of Kings. If you’re excited about any of these games and want to be a part of the community, then this is the group for you!
We’re looking for people who are motivated, enthusiastic, and able to promote games at their local game store and conventions. You’ll find
we are unlike any other volunteer program with our unique properties to promote, fun events to be a part of locally and nationally, and a
rewards program that is UNMATCHED by any other company. Being a part of the program will get you special swag, access to the Legion
forums, points to spend in the CMON store, and much more!
Do you have what it takes to be part of the greatest event staff in the industry? Do you have a love of games and sharing them with others?
Do you dream of creating, organizing, and running demos, tournaments, and massive gaming events? Do you hold yourself to the highest 
standards of having fun? If so, we want to hear from you! Fill out the form below and someone will contact you with details on how to join.
